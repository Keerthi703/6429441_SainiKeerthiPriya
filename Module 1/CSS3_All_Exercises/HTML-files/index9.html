Community Bulletin

The city is planning to host several events over the coming months including a summer festival, farmer's market, and open mic nights. Volunteers are welcome to join planning committees or participate in event setup.

Local businesses are encouraged to sponsor and participate in these events. Booth registrations are now open and can be completed via the portal. Early bird discounts apply until the end of the month.

Community members are also invited to submit their event ideas through our suggestion box. All suggestions will be reviewed during the monthly council meeting.
